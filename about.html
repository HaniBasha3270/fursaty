My Story

Hello! I'm HANI BASHA, the creator behind Fursati - a unified platform designed to revolutionize how students and job seekers discover and apply for opportunities.

The inspiration for Fursati came from observing the challenges faced by countless students and young professionals in my community. I saw talented individuals struggling to navigate through scattered opportunities, complex application processes, and limited access to valuable resources.

With a background in technology and a passion for social impact, I embarked on a mission to create a solution that would simplify this journey. Fursati represents my commitment to empowering the next generation by providing equal access to education, employment, and entrepreneurial opportunities.

"My vision is to create a world where every young person has equal access to opportunities that can transform their lives, regardless of their background or circumstances."

Mission

To democratize access to opportunities by creating a unified platform that simplifies the application process and connects talented individuals with life-changing opportunities.

Vision

A world where geographical and socio-economic barriers no longer limit access to education, employment, and entrepreneurial opportunities for youth worldwide.
