Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common application questions

How long does the application process take?

Most applications take 5-15 minutes to complete, depending on the opportunity type and your preparation level. Having your documents ready beforehand significantly speeds up the process.

What documents do I need to prepare?

Common required documents include:

  • Updated CV/Resume
  • Academic transcripts
  • Identification document
  • Motivation letter template
  • Recommendation letters (if required)

Can I edit my application after submission?

Applications can be edited until the opportunity deadline or until the organization starts reviewing applications. You'll receive notifications about your application status and any required updates.

Is there a limit to how many opportunities I can apply for?

No, there's no limit! You can apply to as many opportunities as you qualify for. We recommend focusing on opportunities that best match your profile and goals for higher success rates.
